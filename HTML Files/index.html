What are Texas Green Tours?

Texas Green Tours is a program at the University of Texas at Austin that aims to educate the UT Austin campus and community about the broad topic of sustainability through hands-on educational programs.
The University of Texas campus strives to advance sustainability efforts on its campus, in its coursework, its research, and its facility and service management practices. The UT Community has achieved great successes in the development and implementation of a wide variety of highly-commendable sustainability focused efforts, including LEED buildings, recycling programs, sustainable food practices, energy and water savings initiatives, renewable and highly-efficiency energy generation, and also including courses, majors, concentrations, study abroad programs, and student groups.

About Our Green Tours

1 Hour Tours
Students, Faculty, and Staff may sign up for 1 hour tours that will provide an overview of UT's sustainable features and programs. The tour starts off at the BELO Center for New Media and ends at the CLA. Times will depend upon tour leaders. However, we will do our best to accomodate any special requests.
K-12 Guided Tours
K-12 schools interested in sustainability may sign up for an interactive tour across campus. Past tours have included presentations from: Jester Gardens, Hot Science Cool Talks, Thermal Labs, and Maker Studios. These tours last all of school day, from 9am-1pm. Time for lunch is included where students can bring their own lunch or buy from Littlefield Cafe. Special requests for different presentations may be accomadated.
Self Guided Tours
To start with the completion of the interactive map, this tour will be created by the user through the interactive map. Participants will be able to click on which sustainable features interest them the most, and the map will generate the best tour option for them.
